What Can I Do For You?


With Tr3umphant.Designs, development for your next project is only a click away. As a personal developer with an ample amount of background experience, I can make your dream business plan, money venture, or even personal portfolio, come to life.

  • 1 First Step - Contact

    By contacting me via through this website, my email, and/or phone number, I can know that you are interested in the service.

  • 2 Second Step - Discussion/Planning

    We now need to iron out all the details of your request. To ensure you are getting the best deal for your project, we need to discuss which plan you choose, when you need it finished, the purpose of your project, and any other details pertaining to such. I take notes of any information provided to ensure nothing is left out of the development process.

  • 3 Third Step - Designing

    The visuals for you app, (or what I refer to them as 'Wireframes'), is all the UI related aspects of your app. This includes colors schemes, buttons, icons, images, etc. I will ensure you like the way the app will look before I start working on your project.

  • 4 Fourth Step - Development

    Now I can start creating your app. The first third of the cost of you project will be due as a deposit. In order to insure you receive your product in a timely fashion, we will have the time estimate discussed in the prior step. In this period, I will be contacting you weekly for questions/updates with your app.

  • 5 Fifth Step - Completion

    At this point, your app is complete, and I will be walking you through the steps to putting your app on a server or market. The second two thirds of the fee for development is due at this point as well.
